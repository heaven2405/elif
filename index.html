Kapalı Hamburger Recipe

🥖 Bread Ingredients

  • 1/2 glass warm water
  • 1/2 glass milk
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 – 3.5 cups flour

🍖 Meat Ingredients

  • 500 g minced beef
  • 1 onion (grated)
  • 3 teaspoons breadcrumbs
  • 3 teaspoons vinegar
  • 3 teaspoons mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper

🍳 Preparation

  1. Mix warm water and yeast and let it activate.
  2. Add milk, yogurt, oil and salt.
  3. Slowly add flour and knead a soft dough.
  4. Let the dough rest for 1 hour.
  5. Mix minced meat with onion and spices.
  6. Shape into burger patties and rest in the fridge for 1 hour.
  7. Bake the bread and cook the patties.
  8. Assemble the hamburger and serve.
